Who we are

Four individuals crossed paths during a charity program with a shared goal of reducing food watage. Backed by Mountain Partners, they joined an accelerator program before founding Grub Cycle.

Since then, Grub Cycle has raised a few rounds of grants and investment worth almost RM1 million while gaining the support of Mountain Partners, MaGIC, myHarapan, Cradle, SUPERB TERAJU, Alliance Bank and Manulife - along with winning two awards at the Alliance BizSmart Challenge 2016.
